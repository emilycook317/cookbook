Cranberry Chicken

In a bowl, stir together:

  • ⅔ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 1 TBS honey
  • 1 tsp dry mustard
  • 1 can or 1½ cup fresh cranberries slightly cooked (or you can use canned)
  • 1 medium onion chopped or sauteed

Place 8-10 pieces of boneless, skinned chicken breasts in a baking dish. Bake at 400° for 25 minutes, uncovered.

Spoon cranberry mixture over chicken. Bake for 20 more minutes.

Goes well with stove top stuffing and baked potatoes.
