Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine noodles
  • 1⁄2 cup butter
  • 1 cup cream
  • 3 cups of finely grated cheese, 2 1⁄2 cups Parmesan and 1⁄2 cup Romano or Asiago (or a mixture of both)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Cook noodles. While they are boiling, add butter and cream to a sauce pan and heat up. Stir in 2 cups of the cheese. When noodles are done, add to sauce along with remaining cheese. Warm it all through and serve.
