Sour Cream Sugar Cookies

Cookie:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1⁄2 tsp almond extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1⁄2 tsp baking soda
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1⁄2 cup sour cream

Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 TBS milk
  • 1 tsp almond extract

For Cookies: Cream butter (1 cup) and sugar (1 cup). Add eggs (2) and the almond extract (1⁄2 tsp). Mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl and add gradually to the wet mixture. Blend in sour cream.

Divide into two parts, place on waxed paper and flatten. Place in ziplock bags and chill at least 5 hours (better overnight) in refrigerator.

Roll out to 1⁄4 inch thick and cut out with cookie cutters. Dough is very sticky; use plenty of flour. Dough is easier to manage when cold.

Bake at 375°F for 6-8 minutes (7 1⁄2 minutes. Take out before the edges brown. If the edges are slightly brown they are overcooked. Leave on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then place on cooling rack to cool. Make 2-3 dozen cookies, depending on size.

For Frosting: Blend ingredients together until smooth. Add food coloring if desired. Mixture will be thick at first.
