Southwest Chicken Salad

Chicken

  • Chicken breast(s)
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Flour
  • Olive oil

Chipotle Ranch Dressing

  • 8 oz sour cream
  • ¾ cup mayo
  • 3 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp dill
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lime juice

Toppings

  • Romaine lettuce
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 can of black beans
  • Cheddar cheese or Mexican crumble cheese (or both!)
  • Avacado
  • Tortilla strips
  • Lime wedges

For Chicken: Cut chicken breast(s) in half. Cover with seran wrap or a ziploc bag and use a meat mallet to pound until thin. Cover each side with cajun seasoning. Then cover each side with flour.

In a skillet on the stove, cover the bottom of the skillet with a thin layer of olive oil and heat on medium-high heat. Add chicken breasts and cook for approximately 4-5 minutes on each side or until each side has a golden crust and is cooked to 165 degrees. Remove from pan and set chicken aside. Slice into bite sized pieces once chicken has cooled a bit.

For Dressing: Add all ingredients to a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.

For the Salad: Wash and cut the romaine lettuce. Drain and rinse the black beans and corn. Prepare all desired salad toppings and serve!
