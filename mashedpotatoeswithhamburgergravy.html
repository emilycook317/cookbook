Mashed Potatoes with Hamburger Gravy

Mashed Potatoes

  • Potatoes
  • Buttermilk
  • Sour Cream
  • Butter, softened
  • Salt & Pepper

Hamburger Gravy

  • 1⁄2 lb. hamburger
  • 2 cups water
  • 3 cubes beef bullion
  • Seasonings
  • 1⁄2 cup cold water
  • 3 TBS cornstarch

For Mashed Potatoes: Boil a large pot of water on the stvoe. Peel potatoes and cut into small cubes. Add potatoes to boiling water and boil until potatoes are soft.

Add potatoes, buttermilk, sour cream, butter, salt, and pepper (all to taste) to a KitchenAid. Beat with the whisk attachment until smooth. Adjust seasonings to taste.

For Hamburger Gravy: Brown hamburger (burn it a little for extra flavor) in a large pan. Add about 2 cups of water and beef bullion cubes. Add any seasonings to taste such as garlic, onion, seasoning salt, lemon pepper, ginger, etc. In a cup, mix 1⁄2 cup cold water and 3 TBS cornstarch. Stir into meat. Bring to a boil. Serve over masked potatoes.
