Barbeque Chicken Grilled Cheese

Recipe Link

BBQ Chicken

  • 2 TBS olive olive
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2½ cups shredded roast chicken
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 12 slices Havarti cheese

Garlic Butter Bread

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp parsley, minced
  • 12 slices sourdough bread or sturdy white bread

BBQ Chicken

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook onions until soft, about 2 minutes. Add garlic (3 cloves) and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add the shredded chicken, smoked paprika (½ tsp), and Worcestershire sauce (1 tsp) and bbq sauce (1 cup). Stir to combine and cook until everything is heated through, about 2-3 minutes.

Garlic Butter Bread

1. Add butter (¼ cup), garlic (2 cloves) and minced parsley (2 tsp) in a medium bowl. Microwave until melted.

2. Lay 12 slices of bread on work surface. Spread melted butter mixture over each piece.

3. Turn each piece over and 6 of the bread slices with 6 of the cheese slices and the bbq chicken mixture, dividing evenly. Add the remaining cheese over the top of the chicken and top with the remaining 6 slices of bread (both the bottom and the top should have the buttered side exposed)

4. Set buttered sandwiches in a large non-stick skillet. Place over medium heat and cook until the bottoms of the sandwiches are browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Turn sandwiches over, reduce heat to medium-low and continue cooking until the bottom is browned and the cheese is melted, 3 to 5 minutes longer.
