Salsa Verde Chicken Nachos

Salsa Verde Chicken

  • 1 16-oz jar of Salsa Verde
  • 2 chicken breasts

Nachos

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Pepper Jack (or other) cheese

Toppings

Guacamole
  • 4 Avacados
  • 2 TBS lime juice
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
  • ¼ cup red onion
  • 3 TBS cilantro, finely chopped
  • ¾-1 jalapeno pepper, discard half the seeds before finely dicing
Half, pit and scoop out the avacado into a medium bowl, cover with lime juice and stir around to coat. Add salt and mash. Stir in the rest of the ingredients.
Black Beans
Sour Cream
Salsa

Place chicken breasts and salsa verde in an instant-pot. Cook on manual for 30 minutes. Release steam and shred chicken.

Make the guacamole and black beans.

Turn the oven on to broil. Line a baking sheet with tortilla chips and cover with cheese. Broil until cheese is melted.

Top nachos with beans, guacamole, sour cream, salsa, etc.
