Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 or 4 boneless, skinless chicken breats
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • 1 cup onions, chopped
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 cup carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 to 3 small potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • ½ cup frozen peas
  • ½ cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 2 refrigerated pie crusts

1. Boil chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces. (Or use the meat from a rotisserie chicken)

2. Melt butter in large skillet; add carrots and potatoes. Cover, cook on medium heat until potatoes start to get soft, about 20 minutes. After potatoes and carrots have softened, add onions and celery and cook several more minutes.

3. Pour flour over veggies, mix.

4. Gradually add broth and half and half. Cook on medium heat until mixture thickens and boils.

5. Add chicken and salt and pepper. Add peas at this stage.

6. Pour into greased 9x13 pan. Top with pie crust. Scrunch the edges and slit the top.

7. Cover with foil. Bake at 400° covered for about 10-15 minutes. Remove foil and cook an additional 20-30 minutes until crust is golden brown.
