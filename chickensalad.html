Dad's Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cubed chicken (such as cooled rotisserie chicken)
  • ½ cup mayonaise
  • ¼ cup Dad's ranch dressing
  • ¼ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 ½ tsp mustard (Dijon or yellow). Don't skip it.
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper

Possible Add-Ins

  • ¾ cup diced tart apple
  • ⅓ cup dried cranberries
  • ¼ cup chopped pecans
  • 2 cups grapes cut in half
  • 2-3 stalks of celery diced
  • ½ tsp celery seed
  • 3 green onions or diced red onion
  • Tarragon
  • Toasted Almonds
  • Lemon Juice
  • 2 TBS Fresh Parsley
  • Dill
  • Shallots
  • Cashews

Combine all ingredients and desired add-ins. Serve on croissants, other bread, or lettuce wraps.
